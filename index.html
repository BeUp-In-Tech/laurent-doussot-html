The Meaning

THE MEANING OF “ONAMI”

Onami means “big wave” in Japanese — a name that reflects the forces that have shaped my work for decades.

The Artist

SCULPTURAL ORIGINS

As a designer, sculptor, and lifelong surfer, I have always respected the power and beauty of the ocean. Its curves, shifting lines, and movement of light influence everything I create.

The Creation

HOW ONAMI WAS CREATED

ONAMI was born from sculpted volumes, natural curvature, and flowing transitions inspired by waves. As light moves across the case, it reveals tension and depth.

A tool watch shaped by nature — refined by hand.

Performance

ENGINEERED FOR THE OCEAN

ONAMI is designed as a true dive watch — not a styling exercise.

With 200 meters of water resistance, a slim Japanese automatic movement, and a case sculpted to perform under pressure, ONAMI balances function with form.

A tool watch shaped by nature, refined by hand, and built for depth.
